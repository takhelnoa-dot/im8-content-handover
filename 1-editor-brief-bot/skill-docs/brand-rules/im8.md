# IM8 Brand Rules

## Exclusions (compliance / regulatory)

**Instant-impact claims are excluded.** Lines like "I felt it immediately," "I noticed the result instantly," "within hours I felt different" must NOT appear in any IM8 brief. They are excluded for compliance reasons (supplements cannot make rapid-onset health claims).

When a usable line abuts an excluded one in the same speaker run, surround the run with a `[NOTE — EDITOR: cut around Lx]` directive showing exactly which line range to splice around.

If a cut would rely heavily on instant-impact lines and there isn't enough sustained-benefit material to back-fill, surface this as an `⚠ EDITOR NOTE` callout under the script header explaining what was excluded.

## Approved positioning anchors

Always reach for these when supported by transcript text. Do not invent them — only deploy when the speaker actually says them or a close paraphrase exists in transcript:

- **NSF Certified for Sport** — independent purity/potency certification
- **Mayo Clinic Ventures partnership** — first time Mayo Clinic has partnered with a supplement company
- **NASA scientists** — Mayo + NASA scientists involved in formulation
- **90+ ingredients at clinical doses** — versus competitors who "sprinkle" sub-clinical amounts
- **No proprietary blend** — every ingredient listed at its actual dose
- **Replaces 15-16 supplements in one packet** — simplification frame
- **Daily Ultimate Essentials clinical study** — 95% improvement in energy in randomised controlled study, plus sleep / digestion / mental clarity gains
- **Founded by David Beckham + Jonathan Skinner** — co-founder authenticity

## Voice / tone

- Confident, not salesy. The product is the answer, not the climax.
- Education / story first, product reveal second.
- Speaker's own words preserved exactly. Do not "polish" their phrasing.
