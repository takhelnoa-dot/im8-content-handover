# Reel Structure Adaptation Rules

The master template's "Suggested Structure" section is a placeholder. It MUST be rewritten so the Reel structure mirrors the ambassador's actual best-performing video flow.

## Inputs available

From `roster.research`:
- `contentStyle.tone`
- `contentStyle.formats[]` (e.g. talking head, vlog, B-roll-led, educational, lifestyle)
- `contentStyle.typicalOpenStyle`
- `contentStyle.typicalCloseStyle`
- `contentStyle.pacing`
- `niche`
- `topicMatch[]`

Plus optional reference-video analysis (if a reference URL was provided): pacing notes, hook structure, CTA structure, energy curve.

## Adaptation rules

For each of the 5 structural beats in the template (Open, Build the case, The one-product truth, The IM8 reveal, Proof / personal experience, CTA), rewrite the directional text so it:

1. **Uses the ambassador's typical opening style for "Open"** — e.g. if they typically open with a question, write a question prompt. If they open with a personal anecdote, write an anecdote prompt. If they open with a contrarian claim, write that.
2. **Uses the ambassador's natural rhythm for "Build the case"** — fast-cut educators get 2-3 short bullet beats; long-form storytellers get one continuous narrative beat.
3. **Adapts the IM8 reveal to their voice** — a clinical creator names ingredients with doses; a lifestyle creator names them as part of a routine; an athlete names the performance benefit.
4. **Personalises "Proof / personal experience"** to their actual life context — a marathoner talks recovery; a doctor cites a patient pattern; an athlete cites training data.
5. **Closes in their voice** — keep the CTA section explicitly "in your voice, no script" — but provide example phrasing that matches their typical close style.

## Anti-patterns

- Do NOT write the structure as a generic supplement-ad template
- Do NOT use staccato fragments unless that's their actual style
- Do NOT use marketing-copy phrasing ("game-changer", "discover", "unlock")
- Do NOT bolt the focus area onto only one section — it must thread through all 5

## Example transformation

If the ambassador is a fitness creator who typically opens with a 5-second pain-point question, builds with 30 seconds of personal experience, and closes with their own conviction line:

**Generic template:**
> Open: pain point or curiosity gap
> Build: education / story / problem-framing
> CTA: in your natural language

**Adapted for this ambassador:**
> Open: ask the painful question your audience whispers but doesn't post — frame it around {focus}. Examples in your voice from your last 3 viral hooks: ["...", "...", "..."]
> Build: one continuous personal-experience beat (~30s). What you tried, what didn't work, what changed. Lean on the {focus}-specific story you already told in [reference video].
> CTA: your closing phrase ("If you take one thing from this..." / "Stop overthinking..." / etc) — drop the discount code naturally
