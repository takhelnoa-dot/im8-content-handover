# DO'S & DON'TS

## Do

- Talk like you're talking to someone you care about — not selling to a viewer
- Keep claims grounded in your own experience and expertise — not general statements
- Mention NSF Certified for Sport — it's a meaningful credibility signal
- Include the discount code at the end of every deliverable
- Tag @im8health in every post and story
- Leave 2–3 seconds of pause between sections in raw footage — makes editing clean
- Use natural light wherever possible
- Film vertical 9:16, in the highest quality your phone or camera supports

## Don't

- Read from a script. If it sounds scripted, start over
- Make the IM8 reveal the climax — the education or story is the climax, the product is the answer at the end of it
- Oversell. Your credibility is the asset — protect it
- Rush the educational sections — your audience slows down when you slow down
- Wear IM8 branding before the product appears on screen
- Use heavy filters or aggressive color grading — keep it natural
- Make claims about specific health outcomes you can't personally vouch for
